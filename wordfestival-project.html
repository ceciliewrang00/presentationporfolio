Prototype Wordfestival

Our second project in the first semester focused on Wordfestival. We were given a case related to book sales, while also considering that the festival aims to be a digital break which challenged us to think outside the box.

Within our group, we divided the tasks between us. I was responsible for creating the prototype and designing the poster, with feedback from the group on minor adjustments. The photos and background illustrations were created by other members of the group.
