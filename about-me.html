About me

My name is Cecilie, I’m 25, and I study Multimedia Design in Odense. I discovered the impact of good design while working in Munich for Oscar Car Rental, where I represented the customer’s voice as a stakeholder.

Curiosity and creativity drive me. I love learning, problem-solving, and exploring new ideas, and I’m passionate about UX, turning creative concepts into practical, user-friendly solutions.
